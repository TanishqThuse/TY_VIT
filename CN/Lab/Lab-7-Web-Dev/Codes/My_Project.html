VIT Project Categories

SDP emphasizes teamwork, coding, and project management. Students develop complete software solutions, learning testing, debugging, and documentation aligned with industry standards.

EDI encourages innovation and design thinking. Students build prototypes, test solutions, and focus on problem-solving with real-world applications.

Design Thinking projects help students empathize with users, brainstorm creative ideas, and design impactful solutions relevant to academics and industry.

Course projects are part of the regular curriculum. They bridge theory with practice and prepare students for research, higher studies, and industry internships.
